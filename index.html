Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 1, 2025

Welcome to the TA Solutions. Our user's privacy is our highest priority. Our philosophy is straightforward: the best way to keep your data safe is to not collect it in the first place.

Our Commitment to Privacy

The core functionality of the TA Solutions application runs locally on your machine. We do not have servers that collect, store, or process any of your personal data or user-generated content.

License Validation & Third-Party Services

To activate and validate your license, the application requires an internet connection to communicate with the Autodesk Entitlement Service. This is a standard procedure for apps sold on the Autodesk App Store.

This validation process verifies your purchase using your Autodesk account information. This communication occurs directly between the application on your computer and Autodesk's secure servers.

We do not receive, collect, or store any of your personal information during this process, such as your name, email, or Autodesk ID. Our application only receives a yes/no confirmation that you have a valid license.

The handling of your data by Autodesk is governed by their privacy policy. We encourage you to review the Autodesk Privacy Statement for complete details.

Changes to This Policy
