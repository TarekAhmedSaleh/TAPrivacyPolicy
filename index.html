Our Commitment to Privacy

The TA Solutions application is engineered to be a completely self-contained tool. It runs locally on your machine, does not require an active internet connection to perform its duties, and does not communicate any information about you or your usage to us or any third party.

  • User Content: Any files, designs, or data that you create or modify using our application. All your work remains your own.
